How to solve the white cross

A cube with 1 color is a center piece
A cube with 2 colors is an edge piece
A cube with 3 colors is a corner piece
Remember to do the same for all center pieces.

  • 1. Align the center colors of the faces with their respective edges
  • 2. Make sure these colors line up before the next step
